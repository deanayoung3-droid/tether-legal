Frequently asked

Where is my data stored?
All your data lives on your iPhone. Tether has no server and no account system.

Are my meal photos uploaded?
They're sent to OpenAI for analysis, then the image is stored locally on your device. OpenAI does not use API-submitted images to train models.

How do I disconnect a service?
Tap your profile icon → Devices → tap "Remove" next to any connected service.

How do I restart onboarding?
Tap your profile icon → scroll to the bottom → tap "Restart Onboarding".

I'm not getting widget data.
Open Tether to refresh data into the App Group store, then the widgets will update.
